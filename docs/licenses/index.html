Licenses and Attribution

Outfinity Quiz instruments have different origins and licenses. This page documents the provenance of each instrument and the terms under which it is published.

Original Instruments

Instruments designed by Outfinity are published under CC BY 4.0 (Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International). You are free to share and adapt them, provided you give appropriate credit.

External Sources

Some instruments draw on published scales or items from the following sources:

  • International Personality Item Pool (IPIP): Items and scales in the public domain for personality, interests, conscientiousness, agreeableness, and leadership.
  • OpenScales Open Repository: Scales under CC0, CC BY, or CC BY-SA with explicit metadata and licenses.
  • Academic CC BY instruments: Used only after verifying the publication and license of each instrument.

Excluded Sources

Instruments with unspecified licenses, "free for personal use," "free for research," CC BY-NC, or copies found on third-party sites are not admitted without written permission.

Attribution

Each quiz page includes a methodology section that identifies the instrument's origin, license, and citation. If you reuse or adapt an instrument, please preserve the attribution.
