# Conflict Signature

## Introduction

The Conflict Signature quiz maps what the user actually does when disagreement becomes real, measuring reflexes across five conflict approaches.

## Core Content

### Source and Licensing

Original Outfinity instrument.

Source: Original
License: CC BY 4.0

### Instrument Design

**Question Count:** 8 forced-choice scenarios
**Response Format:** First and second choice from 5 options
**Duration:** 3 minutes
**Scoring:** First choice = 3 points, second choice = 1 point

### Dimensions (Conflict Approaches)

1. **Confront** - Address conflict directly, seek resolution through discussion
2. **Negotiate** - Seek compromise, find middle ground, trade concessions
3. **Accommodate** - Yield to preserve relationship, prioritize harmony
4. **Avoid** - Withdraw or defer, minimize engagement, wait for resolution
5. **Transform** - Reframe conflict as shared problem, seek win-win solutions

### Viral Mechanics

- **Conflict signature card:** Visual representation of five approaches
- **Challenge a friend:** Share quiz URL without result
- **Comparison overlay:** "Our conflict styles complement/clash in these ways"

### Result Interpretation

Shows primary and secondary conflict approaches with interpretive text describing the user's conflict reflex.

## Conclusion

Validates forced-choice scoring and demonstrates challenge-a-friend viral mechanic.
