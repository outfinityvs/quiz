# Risk Palate

## Introduction

The Risk Palate quiz uses a novel "risk menu" metaphor to map what risks the user would actually accept across different life domains.

## Core Content

### Source and Licensing

Original Outfinity instrument.

Source: Original
License: CC BY 4.0

### Instrument Design

**Question Count:** 10 risk scenarios (2 per dimension)
**Response Format:** 3-option appetite scale (Always accept, Sometimes accept, Never accept)
**Duration:** 2-3 minutes
**Scoring:** Sum of appetite scores per dimension

### Dimensions (Risk Types)

1. **Financial Risk** - Money at stake, investment decisions, financial loss
2. **Reputational Risk** - Public image at stake, social judgment, career impact
3. **Relational Risk** - Relationships at stake, trust, loyalty, social bonds
4. **Existential Risk** - Identity or survival at stake, life-or-death decisions
5. **Creative Risk** - Original work at stake, artistic expression, innovation

### Viral Mechanics

- **Risk menu card:** Visual representation of appetite for each risk type
- **Dare a friend:** Share quiz URL without result
- **Comparison:** "Our risk palates differ in these ways"

### Result Interpretation

Shows risk appetite for each dimension as a "menu card" with selections highlighted. Describes the user's risk tolerance profile.

## Conclusion

Validates three-option Likert scoring and demonstrates novel menu-card result format.
