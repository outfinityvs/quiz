# Collaboration Species

## Introduction

The Collaboration Species quiz maps how the user actually works with others, identifying their natural collaboration style and the team roles they gravitate toward.

## Core Content

### Source and Licensing

Original Outfinity instrument. No external scales used.

Source: Original
License: CC BY 4.0

### Instrument Design

**Question Count:** 10 forced-choice scenarios
**Response Format:** First and second choice from 4 options
**Duration:** 3-4 minutes
**Scoring:** First choice = 3 points, second choice = 1 point

### Dimensions (Species)

1. **Solo Creator** - Works best independently, delivers alone, values autonomy
2. **Dyad Partner** - Works best with one trusted collaborator, deep partnership
3. **Team Player** - Thrives in structured small teams (3-7 people), clear roles
4. **Community Weaver** - Builds trust and connections across groups, facilitates
5. **Mentor/Catalyst** - Enables others to do their best work, guides rather than does

### Viral Mechanics

- **Team Constellation:** Invite 3+ colleagues, see team species distribution
- **Redundant/Absent roles:** Identify which species are over/under-represented
- **Complementarity:** "Your team needs more Community Weavers"

### Result Interpretation

Shows primary and secondary species with interpretive text. Includes team constellation feature for groups.

## Conclusion

Validates forced-choice scoring with first/second preference and demonstrates team constellation viral feature.
