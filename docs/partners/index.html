Partners and Recommendations

Outfinity does not sell answers, profiles, or audience segments. Partners may support a possible next step only when it is relevant to a predefined result. The relationship is declared visibly.

How Recommendations Work

Some quiz results include an optional next step. This recommendation is predefined and selected locally in your browser, based only on the result calculated on your device. Outfinity has not received your answers or profile.

The result is complete without the recommendation. You do not need to follow it.

Relationship Types

Every recommendation is labeled with its relationship type:

  • Outfinity initiative: A project or venture operated by Outfinity.
  • Editorial partner: A partner recommended for editorial reasons, with no commercial relationship.
  • Declared commercial partnership: A partner with a commercial relationship. The nature of the relationship is stated.
  • Affiliate: Outfinity may receive a contribution if you choose this option.

What Partners Cannot Do

Partners cannot modify the instrument, the scoring algorithm, or the interpretation. They do not receive your profile. External links do not include your archetype, score, or any individual identifier.

Opening an external destination is voluntary. Your result is not transmitted automatically.

What We Do Not Do

No carousels of offers, no countdown timers, no artificial scarcity, no preselected forms, and no messages suggesting that your profile has been observed by Outfinity or any partner.
